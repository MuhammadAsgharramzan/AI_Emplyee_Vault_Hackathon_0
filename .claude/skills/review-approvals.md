# Review Approvals

Review and process items in the Pending_Approval folder according to Company Handbook rules.

## Instructions

You are reviewing items that require human approval before action.

1. **Check Pending_Approval folder** for items awaiting review

2. **For each item:**
   - Read the full content and context
   - Check Company_Handbook.md for relevant rules
   - Assess risk level (low/medium/high)
   - Provide recommendation (approve/reject/modify)
   - Explain reasoning clearly

3. **Present to human** in a clear format:
   - Item summary
   - What action is requested
   - Risk assessment
   - Your recommendation
   - Reasoning

4. **Wait for human decision** - DO NOT auto-approve

5. **After human moves file:**
   - If moved to /Approved: Execute the action
   - If moved to /Rejected: Log and archive
   - Update Dashboard with the decision

## Approval Categories

### Financial Actions
- Payments over $500: Always require approval
- New payees: Always require approval
- Recurring payments under $50: Can recommend auto-approve

### Communication Actions
- Emails to new contacts: Require approval
- Bulk emails: Always require approval
- Replies to known contacts: Can recommend auto-approve

### Social Media Actions
- New posts: Require approval
- Scheduled posts: Require approval
- Replies/comments: Assess case-by-case

### File Operations
- Deletions: Always require approval
- Moves outside vault: Require approval
- Internal organization: Can recommend auto-approve

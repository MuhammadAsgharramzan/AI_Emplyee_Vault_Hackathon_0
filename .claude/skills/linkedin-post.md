# Generate LinkedIn Post

Create a professional LinkedIn post about your business to generate leads and engagement.

## Instructions

You are creating a LinkedIn post to promote the business and generate sales leads.

1. **Review business context:**
   - Read Company_Handbook.md for business information
   - Check Dashboard.md for recent activities
   - Review Done folder for completed projects to highlight

2. **Create an engaging post** that:
   - Highlights recent achievements or projects
   - Provides value to the audience
   - Includes a clear call-to-action
   - Uses professional tone
   - Is 150-300 words (LinkedIn optimal length)
   - Includes relevant hashtags (3-5)

3. **Post structure:**
   - Hook: Attention-grabbing opening
   - Value: Share insight, achievement, or lesson
   - Call-to-action: What you want readers to do
   - Hashtags: Relevant industry tags

4. **Save the post** to `need_action/linkedin_posts/LINKEDIN_POST_[date].md`

5. **Set status to 'draft'** for human review before posting

## Content Guidelines

**Do:**
- Share genuine achievements and insights
- Be authentic and professional
- Provide actionable value
- Use storytelling when appropriate
- Include specific results/numbers if available

**Don't:**
- Use overly salesy language
- Make unverifiable claims
- Use too many hashtags (max 5)
- Post without human approval
- Share confidential information

Confirm the post draft was created and provide a preview of the content.
